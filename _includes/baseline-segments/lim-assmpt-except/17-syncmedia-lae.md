### Limitations, Assumptions, or Exceptions

-   Synchronized media includes audio or video synchronized with other formats or timed interactive components; media that is a media alternative for text and clearly labeled as such may be excluded from some checks.
-   Captions and audio descriptions need not be enabled by default but must be available; separate media files may be provided for captioned or audio-described versions.
-   Live caption quality may vary; allowances for lower accuracy in live captions can be considered where real-time limitations apply.
