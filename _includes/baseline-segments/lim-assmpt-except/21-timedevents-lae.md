### Limitations, Assumptions, Exceptions

-   Time limits that are part of real-time or essential activities, or exceed 20 hours, are not included in this test.
-   Changing content presented "in parallel" is defined; controls for pausing/hiding are required unless the movement is essential.
-   Notes from SC 1.4.2 and SC 2.2.2 clarify non-interference and requirements for audio controls and pausing behavior.
