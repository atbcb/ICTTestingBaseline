Test: Captions (Prerecorded) (17.D)

Identify Content

Pre-recorded synchronized multimedia. Do not include media that is clearly labeled as a media alternative for text.

Test Instructions

  1. Enable captions and play the media; if captions are in a separate file, test that file.
  2. Check that captions are provided and that they are accurate, time-synchronized, and equivalent to the audio, including non-dialogue audio information. [SC 1.2.2]
  3. Check that captions do not obscure important visual content. [SC 1.2.2]

Test Results

If any checks fail, Baseline 17.D-CaptionsPrerecorded fails.
