Test: Audio-only Transcript (16.A)

Identify Content

Pre-recorded audio-only content that is not labeled as a media alternative for text.

Test Instructions

  1. Check that the content provides transcript(s) for audio-only content. [SC 1.2.1]
  2. Ensure the transcript is text (not image-only).
  3. Play the audio while referring to the transcript and verify the transcript accurately and completely represents audio content, including relevant sounds and speaker identification.

Test Results

If any checks fail, Baseline Test 16.A-AudioOnlyTranscript fails.
