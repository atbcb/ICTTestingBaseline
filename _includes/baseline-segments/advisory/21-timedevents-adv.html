Advisory: Tips for streamlined test processes

- Remind testers that when the time-out occurs, visible focus should shift to the time-out alert to comply with success criteria for keyboard accessibility and focus order. - In some cases, it may be necessary to contact the application authors to clarify the conditions under which time-outs occur. - A failure of SC 1.4.2 or 2.2.2 would also fail Conformance Requirement 5: Non-Interference and should be highlighted in test reports. - Browsers must be configured to disable autoplay of audio prior to testing of content. - Content found non-conformant with SC 2.2.2 may be marked for further review for a Section 508 exception if the auto-update is essential.
